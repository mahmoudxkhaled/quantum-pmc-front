QPMC offers a vast range of professional services in the context of Project Controls, including Cost Control, Planning, Progress Monitoring, Quantity Surveying, and Documents Management. Those services were designed to fit into a large variety of organizational structures, and can be tailored further as needed based on the requirements and budget of our Clients.

Most of our services listed below can be offered and applied on either the Company level and/or any Project level depending on the necessity.

1. Cost Management

  • Design, develop and/or physically implement a robust cost management system, and/or support in the restructuring of existing cost management systems.
  • Provide solutions for financial and cost reporting adjustments, including cost breakdown structures, budget allocation, and variance analysis.
  • Support in the development and/or monitoring of the project's cost control strategy with comprehensive cost tracking and reporting mechanisms.
  • In addition, and most importantly, support and/or develop a wide range of cost management solutions from our Client's perspective.

2. Time & Progress Management

  • Develop and manage project master schedules, including critical path analysis and resource allocation planning.
  • Handle schedule revisions and updates based on project changes, delays, and scope modifications.
  • Implement detailed progress management systems with KPI reporting and performance tracking mechanisms.
  • Provide support for schedule optimization and recovery planning when projects face delays or disruptions.

3. Quantity Surveying

  • Develop and manage Bills of Quantities (BOQ) for construction and infrastructure projects with accurate measurements and pricing.
  • Update BOQs based on revised designs, variation orders, and project changes throughout the construction lifecycle.
  • Provide support for pricing, cost estimation, and tender preparation with comprehensive quantity take-offs.
  • Conduct regular quantity surveys and measurements to ensure accurate progress reporting and payment applications.

4. Project Controls Systems Development

  • Develop integrated project controls systems that combine cost management with other functions like planning and change management.
  • Create automated mapping systems between Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and Cost Breakdown Structure (CBS) for seamless integration.
  • Operate and maintain document management systems for project documentation, drawings, and correspondence.
  • Provide training and support for project teams to effectively utilize project controls systems and tools.

5. Project Management Staff Support

  • Provide solutions for clients with staffing shortages by deploying experienced project management personnel.
  • Offer project management staff with suitable experience and seniority levels to match project requirements.
  • Available on weekly or monthly rates with flexible engagement terms to meet varying project needs.
  • Support project teams with temporary or long-term staffing solutions for specialized project controls functions.
